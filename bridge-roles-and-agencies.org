* Strategic Application Tips

- **The M&S Leverage:** Always highlight your 7 years of service experience in your opening sentence. It proves you are reliable and can handle high-pressure user environments.
- **Project over Degree:** Since you left your degree, lead with your =growing-wiki= and =dotfiles= projects. These prove you have the /skills/ the degree was supposed to teach.
- **Remote-First Boards:** Ensure you have active profiles on [[https://otta.com/][Otta]] and [[https://cord.co/][Cord]], as they prioritize technical proficiency and personal projects.
